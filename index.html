Who we are ?

F4 has Well Equipped Machine Shop, Fabrication and Welding shop with sophisticated Equipment’s such as Heavy/Light Duty Lathe Machines, Horizontal/Vertical Boring Machines, Heavy Duty Plano Milling Machine , Universal Milling Machine , Radial and Pillar type of Drilling Machines, Hacksaw/Band Saw Cutting Machines with Tig/Mig and ARC Welding Machines, Oxy Acetylene/Flame cutting equipment’s etc.
