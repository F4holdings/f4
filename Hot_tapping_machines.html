Hot Tapping Machines

Hot Tapping Machine is a portable under pressure drilling machine with a hydraulic drive (Pneumatic drive is also available upon request). These machines are used for taking bypass branches without shuttingdown the pipeline Flow/Pressure/Temperature for maintenance on the pipeline. This is applicable to any Liquid/Gas transmission pipelines in the Process Industries, Oil And Gas, Water, Sewage etc.

Pressure Balancing Machine

F4 Manufactures Hot Tapping Machines with a Pressure Balancing Machine having Compensation Cylinders, that equalize the pipeline as well as Sea Pressure to reduce stress on the internal components, extending the Tapping Machine’s life. These Hot Tapping Machines are used on High Pressure or for Sub-sea Tapping Operations.

Tapping Range 3 Inch - 36 Inch

Tri Bar Machine (Low Pressure Tapping)

F4 manufactures Tri Bar Hot Tapping Machines that are lightweight and portable. These Hot/Cold Tapping Machines are suitable for Low Pressure Utility and AC chilled water pipeline Tapping’s in order to take a bypass or to make branches. These machines could be used in areas where there are space constraints such as malls, hotels & data centres etc. Due to its Lightweight and compact design. It can be installed manually without the help of any lifting devices, including moving up in the high rise buildings to any floor using the lift. F4 Manufactures state of the art compact Hot Tapping Machines that will be useful to repair or replace the damaged pipeline lines without any shutdown.
