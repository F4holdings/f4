HSEQ

At F4 INDUSTRIAL & PIPELINE ENGINEERS (INDIA) PRIVATE LIMITED. subsidiary of F4 HOLDINGS (INDIA) PRIVATE LIMITED we pursue to deliver products and services that meet or exceed the requirements of our customers in the Oil and Gas sector and Allied Industries like Refineries, (Petro) Chemical, Fertilizers and Process Plants; Utilities Sector; Power Sector; Construction & Infrastructure Sector.


We are Committed:

• To establishing a strong HSEQ Management System for our Operations.
•To provide a safe work environment for our employees, our contractors and other third parties working with us.
• To continuously improve our operations and meet our company’s objectives and targets.
• To abide by the law of the land and the statutory requirements of the place where we operate.
• To undertake programs and actions to maintain consistency in the quality of product & services delivered; protect the environment and to empower our    employees to work safely.
• To provide adequate resources and infrastructure to achieve the targeted HSEQ Performance.
• To periodically review its HSEQ performance and take measures to sustain or further improve its levels.
•To selecting capable supply chain partners and associates and aligning their objectives to that of the company’s objectives.
• To ensure effective communication of company’s policy to its customers, its employees, its partners and associates, and to other interested parties.
• To protect the best interest of the customers, the employees, the contractors, the associates and the partners and the interests of the country where the    company has its businesses.
